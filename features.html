About SoilSmart

SoilSmart is a state-of-the-art AI-powered agriculture assistant designed to help modern farmers maximize their yield by understanding their soil and crop health. We combine real-time sensor data with advanced machine learning to provide actionable, precision-farming insights.

📊 Real-Time Soil Analytics

Our platform allows you to monitor critical soil metrics including Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium, Moisture, pH, and Temperature. View live updates directly on your dashboard to ensure optimal growing conditions and avoid nutrient deficiencies before they impact your yield.

🤖 AI Crop Recommendation

Stop guessing. Our Random Forest Machine Learning model takes your precise soil composition and automatically recommends the absolute best crop to plant (like Rice, Wheat, or Maize). This maximizes your farm's profitability and reduces the risk of crop failure.

📸 AI Crop Scanner & Disease Detection

Notice something wrong with your leaves? Simply snap a photo with your mobile phone and upload it. Our vision AI instantly detects diseases like Leaf Blast or Early Blight, and provides a clear, actionable solution—like adjusting your watering schedule or applying the correct fertilizer—to save your yield.
